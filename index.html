Who am I?

At Skidmore College, I studied Computer Science, with a minor in Religious Studies. Computer Science is a natural fit for me,as I had been curious about the technology around the house from a young age. I have tutored children in computer programming, algebra, and theater. (It is wonderful to see students gain confidence in their abilities.) I have open source programming projects on my github, including esoteric programming languages and large number generators. I showcase these projects on my website, where you can read about them, and try them out. Links to both of these can be found on my resume. When the weather is nice, I can be found photographing birds and other wildlife, which I share to Instagram and Flickr.
